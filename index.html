Coding and Innovation at UCU

Uganda Christian University (UCU) is at the forefront of nurturing technological talent through hands-on coding challenges. With a strong emphasis on computing and technology programs, UCU provides students with opportunities to tackle real-world issues using programming, AI, and software engineering skills. These initiatives foster creativity, teamwork, and entrepreneurial spirit.
